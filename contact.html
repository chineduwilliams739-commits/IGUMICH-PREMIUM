Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services

What safety standards do your products meet?

All our products meet or exceed international safety standards including ANSI (American National Standards Institute), OSHA (Occupational Safety and Health Administration), NIOSH (National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health), and CE (Conformité Européenne) markings. We provide certification documents with every product.

Do you offer bulk order discounts?

Yes, we offer significant discounts for bulk orders and corporate accounts. Discounts vary based on order volume and product type. For a customized bulk quote, please contact our sales team via WhatsApp, email, or the contact form above.

What is your delivery time within Nigeria?

For Lagos: 1-2 business days
For other major cities: 3-5 business days
For remote areas: 5-7 business days
We provide tracking information for all orders. Express delivery options are available for urgent needs.

Do you provide safety training with products?

Yes, we offer complimentary basic safety training for bulk orders and corporate clients. For comprehensive training programs, we have certified safety trainers who can conduct on-site or virtual training sessions. Contact us to discuss your training needs.

What is your return and warranty policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for defective products (unused, in original packaging). Most products come with a 1-year manufacturer's warranty. Specific warranty details are provided with each product. For warranty claims, contact our support team with your purchase details.
