Our Industrial Journey

Founded in 2012, IGUMICH INT'L LIMITED began with a vision to provide comprehensive equipment solutions across multiple industries in West Africa. What started as a specialized safety equipment supplier has grown into a multi-sector industrial solutions provider.

We've expanded our expertise to include marine equipment, construction tools, and industrial machinery, becoming a one-stop solution for businesses across diverse sectors. From construction sites to offshore platforms, manufacturing plants to industrial facilities, we provide the equipment that powers industries.

Our growth is driven by our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer-focused solutions. We don't just supply equipment; we provide complete industrial solutions tailored to each client's specific operational needs and challenges.
